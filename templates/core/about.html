Our Story

Built to Close the Gap Between Donors and Patients

Blood Finder started with a simple frustration: when a hospital needs a specific blood group urgently, finding the right donor in time is still done through phone chains and word of mouth. We built a platform to make that search instant.

Our Mission

Make finding a compatible, willing blood donor as fast as searching for a restaurant nearby — no phone trees, no guesswork.

How We Verify Trust

Donor contact details are only visible to registered, logged-in members, and every donor controls their own availability status.

Community First

Every feature — search, requests, notifications — is designed around one outcome: connecting a willing donor to someone who needs them, quickly.
