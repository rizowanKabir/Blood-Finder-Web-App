Privacy Policy

Last updated: {% now "F Y" %}

1. Information We Collect

When you register, we collect your name, email, phone number, and optional profile picture. If you register as a donor, we additionally collect your age, gender, blood group, weight, location (division, district, area), and donation history. This information is provided directly by you.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your details to operate the core features of Blood Finder: showing your donor profile to people searching for compatible blood, notifying you about nearby blood requests that match your blood group, and letting you manage your own dashboard and donation history.

3. Who Can See Your Information

Your donor profile (name, blood group, general area, and availability) is visible to other registered members. Full contact details such as your phone number are only shown to logged-in users, not to the public internet. You can mark yourself unavailable at any time to pause visibility.

4. Data Storage and Security

Passwords are never stored in plain text — they're hashed using Django's built-in password hashing. We apply standard safeguards including CSRF protection, secure session cookies in production, and access controls that restrict editing to your own profile and records.

5. Your Rights

You can edit or remove your donor profile at any time from your dashboard. To fully delete your account and associated data, contact us using the form on our Contact page.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the platform evolves. Continued use of Blood Finder after changes means you accept the revised policy.

7. Contact Us

Questions about this policy can be sent through our Contact page.
